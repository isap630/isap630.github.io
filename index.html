Hi there :)

As a current student majoring in Emerging Media and minoring in Computer Science, I have developed and continued to grow my passion for digital media, technology, and design. Over the past few years I have been able to learn a variety of design skills and tools including Adobe Creative Cloud, Front-End Coding Languages, and UX/UI skills.

Besides my interest in User Interface Design, I am a photographer and scenic view lover. Appreciating simple things and living in the moment in life is valuable to me, (which is understandable as someone from a busy city lol) therefore I especially love capturing simplicity. I love walking and exploring around the city and capturing small details that some people might overlook due to the busyness of living in New York. My work is a reflection of my views as I like to create and capture simple things that are still able to be appreciated by anyone.
